How To Start Your Own Home Agriculture

Potato for agriculture at home

Step 1

Peel The Potato

Peel the potato and keep the potato in a bowl to use for composting and make fertilisers to plant seeds later on

Step 2

Fertilizing the soil

Add the peels from the potato to the ground or soil, with ground coffee and let decompose for two weeks which will fertilize the soil for planting

Step 3

Let the soil turn rich with nutrients

Two weeks later, the soil turning to a rich brown color is an indication that the soil is ready for planting your seed or a potato

Step 4

Planting the seed or a potato

Add the seed or a potato to the soil and cover it with the soil and water it overtime until it starts growing

Step 5

Let it grow

let the plant grow overtime, Patience is the key while planting. Water it and let proper sunlight get to it

Step 6

Time to harvest & repeat

wait for the right window until Its time to harvest your fruit or vegetable ready to be eaten and repeate the process over again for sustainable ad affordable agriclture at home

Access To Fresh Healthy Produce

Home farming allows you to plant and grow your own fresh fruit or vegetable, giving you a much healthier, nutrient-rich diet

Cost Saving

Home agriculture can help you save some extra money on groceries since you can grow your own at home instead of purchasing it

Environmental benefits

Home agriculture can be done using organic and Sustainable methods by fertilising and regrowing your fruits and veggies which is better than mass producing and use of chemically grown vegetables

Improved mental and physical help

Gardening and agriculture at home has shown to have a good effect on the mental health by keeping stress and anxiety away while also improving your mood and physicality when performing various planting tasks
